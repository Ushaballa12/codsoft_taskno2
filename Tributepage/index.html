Rahul Attuluri

Cofounder, CEO - NxtWave

Biography

Rahul Attuluri, a 32-year-old entrepreneur from Hyderabad, Telangana, is spearheading a groundbreaking venture in software education. As the co-founder of NxtWave Disruptive Technologies, he and his team, composed of IIT alumni, established the region's largest software training institute in 2020.

Their innovative approach, highlighted by programs like CCBP 4.0, emphasizes continuous capacity building and skill development. Rooted in his Telugu heritage and Hindu upbringing, Rahul's leadership is driving transformative change in the tech landscape, positioning NxtWave at the forefront of the industry's evolution.

With a Bachelor's degree in Technology from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Rahul embarked on a visionary journey to revolutionize the landscape of software training. In 2020, fueled by his passion for technology and education, Rahul co-founded NxtWave with fellow IIT alumni.

At the heart of NxtWave's success lies its unique approach to continuous capacity building, epitomized by programs like CCBP 4.0. Through these initiatives, Rahul and his team are not merely imparting knowledge but fostering a culture of perpetual growth and evolution.

Outside of his entrepreneurial pursuits, Rahul is actively involved in community initiatives aimed at promoting STEM education among underprivileged youth. His commitment to social responsibility underscores his dedication to making a positive impact beyond the realms of business.
